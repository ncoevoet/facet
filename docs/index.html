Score, cull and browse your photos, entirely on your own machine

Facet rates every photo, throws out the bursts, blinks and near-duplicates, and gives you a fast private gallery you can search in plain words and browse by the people in it. No cloud, no accounts, no subscriptions.

Get it on GitHub Quick start

Two tools in one

A private gallery to browse and explore your whole library — and a culling workflow to keep only the shots that shine.

Self-hosted gallery

Browse & explore

A full self-hosted gallery to organize and explore your whole library — plus the quality scoring and ranking most galleries leave out.

  • Search in plain words — "sunset on the beach" finds the photo
  • Recognizes people and suggests who to merge
  • Timeline, map, folders, albums & themed slideshows
  • Stays fast even at 100,000+ photos
Culling workflow

Score & cull

A free, fully local culling workflow — with scoring that learns your taste.

  • Rates every photo and picks the keepers
  • Groups bursts, look-alikes & scenes, flags the best of each
  • Per-face blink & expression badges
  • A/B comparison trains a personal "My Taste" ranking
  • One-click auto-cull with a safe dry-run preview

What it does for you

Smart photo tools that run entirely on your machine — a graphics card is optional.

Finds your best shots

Rates every photo's quality and picks the keepers, so you don't scroll through thousands.

Search in plain words

Type "sunset on the beach" and get the photo — no manual tagging needed.

Spots good composition

Highlights well-framed shots — rule of thirds, leading lines and more.

Recognizes people

Groups your photos by who's in them and learns the names you give.

Knows what's in each photo

Auto-labels scenes and subjects so you can filter your whole library instantly.

Locks onto the subject

Tells the subject from the background to judge focus and framing.

Runs on your machine

Works on a laptop CPU; a graphics card just makes it faster and sharper.

Fits your workflow

Plays nicely with Lightroom, darktable and digiKam via standard sidecar files.

Uploads from your phone

Point PhotoSync or any WebDAV app at the built-in inbox — new shots are scored the moment they arrive.

Culls on the developed look

Preview RAWs through your own darktable styles right in the culling view, instead of judging flat previews.

Names your moments

Recognizes beach days, concerts and celebrations on its own — naming scenes, filtering the gallery and powering slideshows.

Ready to share

Proofing links for clients, subject-framed social crops, a portfolio site from any album — even a feed for smart photo frames.

Quick start with Docker

Pick your hardware, copy two lines. One image serves every profile — it runs on a plain CPU with no graphics card.
